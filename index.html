LIVELINK

LIVELINK was designed to cut through the noise—connecting artists directly with venues, businesses, and planners who need live entertainment. With LIVELINK, we’re making it easier to book talent and helping musicians focus on what matters most—their music. Our platform streamlines the entire booking process, reducing the hours spent on promotion, emails, and negotiations.

For musicians, it means more time performing and less time searching for gigs. For venues and event organizers, it means quick access to the perfect talent, tailored to their needs. Whether you're planning a large-scale event or looking for a unique sound for a small gathering, LIVELINK offers a seamless experience for both sides of the stage.

We're not just building connections—we're creating opportunities.

Get Discovered and Booked

LIVELINK helps you reach the right audience faster. Artists can showcase their talents to venues and event planners, who can easily send booking requests. It's a reason to celebrate when your inbox lights up with opportunities!

Gain visibility & coverage

Forget about the endless emails and follow-ups. With LIVELINK, the booking process is streamlined, making it easier for both artists and organizers to set up performances and manage schedules all in one place.

Grow Your Network

Through LIVELINK, artists and event organizers can form lasting connections. After booking a gig, stay connected with venues, event planners, and other professionals to expand your network for future collaborations.

Maximize Opportunities

For musicians, LIVELINK opens up a world of new opportunities. From securing more live performances to gaining visibility with venues, businesses, and event planners—LIVELINK is your platform to grow your presence in the industry.

The Vision Behind LIVELINK: Meet the Innovator

A classically trained pianist and vocalist, Alexandra Plastino studied at Berklee College of Music, where she discovered her passion for empowering artists and connecting them with opportunities. With a deep love for music and a vision to streamline the entertainment booking process, Plastino founded LIVELINK—a dynamic platform that bridges the gap between musicians and event organizers. Dedicated to helping artists focus on their craft and thrive in the industry, Alexandra is on a mission to make the dream of live performance accessible and effortless for all.
